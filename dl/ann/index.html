Formulas

Each neuron performs:

\[ z = \sum (w_i \cdot x_i) + b \]

\[ a = \sigma(z) \]

Where \( \sigma \) is the activation function (e.g., sigmoid, ReLU)

The output is:

\[ \hat{y} = \sigma(w \cdot a + b) \]
